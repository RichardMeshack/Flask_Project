ABOUT US

AGRO-CONNECT is a platform that is aim at improving the productivity in the agricultural sector through the following:

  • Connecting farmers across the globe to share ideas on latest improvement in the agro sector, and also to solve challanges affecting the agricultural sector together.
  • To also connect farmer and consumer for easy access of farm product.
  • Updates on latest new and invention in the agro space. And also allowing farmers to advertise their product.
  • Creation of job oppurtunity by connecting farmer and labourers.

AIM:

To improve productivity and consumption in the agricultural sector both locally and globally.
